Alfredo

Description:

Alfredo is a collaborative studio album by American rapper Freddie Gibbs and American hip hop producer The Alchemist.

It was released on May 29, 2020 and was met with widespread critical acclaim scoring an 88 on Metacritic and debuting on number 15 on Billboard 200.
